SVG Notes
  • - Benefits
    • - OMG so many
    • - support in IE9+, and all other modern-ish browsers
    • - IE9-11 doesn't always play by the same rules, unfortunately
    • - In safari/chrome/ff/opera svg acts almost exactly as you'd expect images to act, inline and as bg, IE makes a bit more complicated, but once you know how IE behaves, it's not too hard to work around
  • - Drawbacks
    • - no IE8, flatout
    • - they don't necessarily play nice as uploaded content into CMS
    • - There are spriting options available, but not
  • - Go over bg, inline,

  • - snap.js / svg.js - libraries for on the fly svg creation and manipulation
